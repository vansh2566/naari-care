Nutrition & Health Guides

Personalized nutrition recommendations for every phase of your journey.

Recommended Nutrition Plans

During Your Period

Leafy Greens

Benefits: Rich in iron to combat fatigue and replenish blood loss.

Recommended: Spinach, kale, broccoli

Serving: 2-3 cups daily

Iron Calcium Fiber

Dark Chocolate

Benefits: Reduces cramps and improves mood through magnesium.

Recommended: 70% cocoa or higher

Serving: 1-2 small squares

Magnesium Antioxidants

Nuts & Seeds

Benefits: Omega-3 fatty acids reduce inflammation and pain.

Recommended: Almonds, walnuts, flaxseeds

Serving: 1 handful daily

Omega-3 Vitamin E

During Pregnancy

Eggs

Benefits: High-quality protein and choline for baby's brain development.

Recommended: Fully cooked eggs

Serving: 1-2 eggs daily

Protein Choline Vitamin D

Dairy Products

Benefits: Essential calcium for bone development.

Recommended: Milk, yogurt, paneer

Serving: 3-4 servings daily

Calcium Protein Vitamin B12

Avocado

Benefits: Folate prevents neural tube defects.

Recommended: Fresh avocado

Serving: 1/2 avocado daily

Folate Healthy Fats

Postpartum Care

Fatty Fish

Benefits: DHA supports baby's brain development through breast milk.

Recommended: Salmon, sardines (low mercury)

Serving: 2-3 times per week

DHA Omega-3 Protein

Whole Grains

Benefits: Sustained energy and improved milk production.

Recommended: Oats, quinoa, brown rice

Serving: 3-4 servings daily

Fiber B Vitamins

Hydration

Benefits: Essential for milk production and recovery.

Recommended: Water, coconut water, herbal teas

Serving: 10-12 glasses daily

Electrolytes

Quick Nutrition Tips

Supplements

Consult your doctor about iron, folic acid, and vitamin D supplements.

Avoid

Limit caffeine, processed foods, and excessive sugar intake.

Balance

Eat a variety of colorful fruits and vegetables daily.

Timing

Eat small, frequent meals to maintain energy levels.
