Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Is Naari Care free to use?

Yes! We offer a free version with essential features. Premium features are available through our subscription plans.

Is my data secure and private?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption and never share your personal health data without your explicit consent.

Can I use Naari Care during pregnancy?

Yes! We have specialized features for pregnancy tracking, nutrition guidance, and weekly updates on your baby's development.

Is Naari AI a replacement for a doctor?

No. Naari AI provides support and information but is not a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult your healthcare provider.

Which platforms is Naari Care available on?

We're currently available on iOS and Android. A web version is coming soon!

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account anytime from Settings > Privacy > Delete Account. All your data will be permanently removed.
